Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an entry fee or cover charge?

Yes, there is usually a small cover charge, especially on weekends or during special events. It helps us support our DJs and performers. Prices may vary depending on the night—feel free to call or check our social media for details.

Do you allow walk-ins or is it reservation only?

We welcome walk-ins, but on busy nights (especially Fridays and Saturdays), we recommend making a reservation to ensure you get a good spot—especially for VIP or lounge areas.

Is the club open every day or only on weekends?

Yes, we’re open every night, but weekends tend to be the most lively with featured DJs, themed parties, and packed dance floors.

What's the age requirement to enter?

You must be 18 years or older to enter.Our staff at the entrance may ask for your valid identification before letting you enter the club.

Do you serve food as well or just drinks?

We serve a variety of snacks and bar bites, including both local and international favorites-perfect to fuel your night out. Enjoy our delicious menu alongside your favorite beverages.

Is there a bar menu or cocktail list I can see?

Yes,Our menu is available both physically and online.You can scan a QR code at your table or ask your bartender or waiter for recommendations.

Do you have any vegeterian or vegan snacks?

Yes, We offer vegeterian and vegan-friendly options such as spring rolls, spicy peanuts, fries, momo (dumplings) with plant based fillings as well as popcorn.

What kind of music do you play?

We play a mix of EDM, techno, Afrobeat, commercial hits ,reggaetonn and some local Nepali fusion beats.The vibe depends on the night and the DJ!

Is there a dance floor?

Of course! We have a dedicated dance floor, and it's usually packed with energy, especially after 11PM.

Are there any special theme nights or events coming up?

Yes! We host theme parties, DJ nights, ladies nights, and holiday events.Follow us on instagram or Facebook to stay updated or You can even check at the Upcoming Events section of our website.You can easily find our facebook and instagram link at he bottom of the page.

Can I take photod or videos?

Yes, feel free to take photos or videos-but please be mindful of pther guest's privacy.We also have photographers on site.

Is there a dress code?

We're chil, but we love a stylish crowd! Avoid flip-flops.Smart casual or trendy attire is recommended.

DO you have VIP or Lounge area?

Yes, We offer VIp seating and private lounges for small groups and celebrations.You can book in advance for bottle service and a more exclusive experience.

Do you host private parties or events?

Definetly! Whether it's a birthday, bachelor/bachelorette, or corporate party, we can help you organize a memorable night.Contact us for packages.You can look at the bottom of the page for our contact information.

Is smoking allowed inside or is there a smoking area?

Smoking inside the club is allowed but no drinking or smoking activity is allowed on the dance floor.

Do you offer parking or valet service?

We have limited parking available. On busy nights, we recommend arriving early or using a taxi. Valet service is not available at the moment.

Is the place safe for solo travelers, especially women?

Yes, we prioritize safety for all our guests.Our security staff is always present, and our team is trained to handle any issues respectfully.

Do you provide taxi ir ride service assistance after closing?

Yes,we can help youi book a reliable taxi when you're ready to head out. Just ask at the front desk or speak to any staff member.

Do you have security or bouncers on-site?

Yes, for your safety and comfort, we have trained security personnel at the entrance and inside the venue at all times.

Where are you located?

We are located at Centre Point, Lakeside Pokhara.
